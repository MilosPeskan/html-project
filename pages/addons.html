Window boards, fascias, reveal liner

uPVC window boards, fascias, and reveal liners play crucial roles in enhancing the aesthetics and functionality of windows and doors.

Window boards provide a neat and durable surface beneath windows, protecting against moisture and offering a finishing touch to interior spaces.

Fascias are essential for covering and protecting the edges of roofs and external walls, ensuring a clean and uniform appearance to the building's exterior.

Reveal liners, on the other hand, help to conceal the rough openings around windows and doors, creating a smooth transition between the frame and the wall.

Made from high-quality uPVC, these components are known for their low maintenance requirements, weather resistance, and longevity, making them popular choices for both residential and commercial applications.


Trims, soffits, architraves

uPVC trims, soffits, and architraves are essential components that add both aesthetic appeal and functionality to building exteriors and interiors.

Trims serve as protective barriers, covering the gaps and joints where different building materials meet, ensuring a clean and finished look.

Soffits are installed beneath the roof overhangs, providing ventilation and protecting the underlying structures from moisture and pests.

Architraves, on the other hand, frame doorways and windows, enhancing the architectural detail and overall design of interior spaces.

Crafted from high-quality uPVC, these components offer excellent durability, weather resistance, and low maintenance requirements.

They are available in a variety of styles, colors, and finishes, allowing for customization to match any architectural style or design preference.

With their combination of practicality and aesthetic versatility, uPVC trims, soffits, and architraves are popular choices for both new construction and renovation projects.


Roller blinds, venetian blinds

uPVC roller blinds and Venetian blinds are popular window treatments that offer both practicality and style. Roller blinds are sleek and versatile, providing a simple yet effective way to control light and privacy in any room. Their smooth operation and low-profile design make them ideal for modern interiors. Venetian blinds, on the other hand, feature horizontal slats that can be tilted to control the amount of light entering a room, offering a timeless and elegant look.
